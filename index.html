The Haggis Sanctuary

Rescuing Haggis since 1982

Our Work Includes

  • Rescue & Shelter: We respond to cases of neglect and abuse, bringing animals out of dangerous situations and into our loving arms.
  • Medical Care: From routine check-ups to life-saving surgeries, we ensure that every animal receives the veterinary care they need to recover and thrive.
  • Adoption Services: Our goal is to match every animal with the perfect forever family, helping them find a loving home where they can live happily.
  • Education & Advocacy: We believe in empowering our community to make a difference. We offer resources on responsible pet ownership, humane treatment of animals, and ways to help prevent animal cruelty.
