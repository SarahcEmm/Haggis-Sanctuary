About Us

At The Haggis Sanctuary, based in the Scottish Highlands our mission is simple: to protect, rescue, and care for animals in need. Founded in 1982, we are a dedicated team of animal lovers and advocates committed to improving the lives of vulnerable animals through rescue efforts, rehabilitation, and finding loving forever homes.

Every year, thousands of animals suffer from neglect, abuse, and abandonment. That’s why we’ve made it our goal to provide a safe haven for these animals, offering them the medical care, nutrition, and love they deserve. Through our network of volunteers, foster homes, and adoption partners, we work tirelessly to ensure every animal in our care has a second chance at life.

Our Work Includes

  • Rescue & Shelter: We respond to cases of neglect and abuse, bringing animals out of dangerous situations and into our loving arms.
  • Medical Care: From routine check-ups to life-saving surgeries, we ensure that every animal receives the veterinary care they need to recover and thrive.
  • Adoption Services: Our goal is to match every animal with the perfect forever family, helping them find a loving home where they can live happily.
  • Education & Advocacy: We believe in empowering our community to make a difference. We offer resources on responsible pet ownership, humane treatment of animals, and ways to help prevent animal cruelty.

We are proud to say that thanks to the generosity of our supporters, we have helped 25 million animals find their forever homes and provided care for countless others. But there is still much more to be done.

At The Haggis Sanctuary, we envision a world where every animal is valued and loved. With your support, we can continue saving lives, one animal at a time.
